# 02 Field Observation Data
## Hargeisa – 26 June & 31 May Districts

---

## 1. Assessment Details
- Location: Hargeisa  
- Districts: 26 June, 31 May  
- Duration: 8 Days  
- Type: Environmental Sanitation Assessment  

---

## 2. Observations

| Category | Status | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Waste Bins | Insufficient | Limited number in public areas |
| Waste Tanks | Not Available | No structured community tanks |
| WASH Facilities | Limited | Lack of handwashing stations |
| Private Companies | Inconsistent | Uneven service coverage |

---

## 3. Field Notes
- Drainage blockages observed  
- Informal dumping areas identified  
- Waste accumulation near markets and transport areas  
- Lack of organized waste disposal systems  

---

## 4. Cleanliness Assessment

| Score | Meaning |

---

## 5. Initial Recommendations
- Increase waste bins  
- Establish waste tanks  
- Improve drainage systems  
- Strengthen waste collection monitoring
