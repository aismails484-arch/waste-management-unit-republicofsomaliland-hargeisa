Hargeisa Waste Management Assessment
Overview
This repository presents a pre-assessment and analytical study of urban environmental sanitation and waste management in Hargeisa, within the context of the Republic of Somaliland.
The project is developed to support evidence-based planning, institutional coordination, and policy development in the area of urban sanitation.
Geographical Scope
The analysis is conducted within the Republic of Somaliland, with a specific focus on Hargeisa City.

Objectives
The main objectives of this assessment are:
To evaluate waste management infrastructure
To identify sanitation and environmental risk areas
To assess service coverage and operational gaps
To support strategic planning and decision-making
To contribute to policy and coordination frameworks
Methodology
The assessment is based on field observation and environmental inspection, conducted over a defined period.
Methods applied include:
Direct field observation
Environmental sanitation assessment
Identification of waste disposal practices
Rapid cleanliness scoring system
Analysis of service gaps and risk areas
